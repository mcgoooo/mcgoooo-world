# Background
After the failure of *What’s My BPM* and still needing a portfolio piece, I decided to build another app.

###### CleanYourMusic
I have around 400 playlists, all sourced from Spotify.  
When using DJ software like Rekordbox or Serato, managing this is overwhelming — especially with 30,000+ songs.  

Deleting or pruning music is tedious across services, and making DJ playlists becomes time-consuming. I want an easy way to:
- Delete tracks from playlists
- Delete entire playlists quickly

Example: I used [TuneMyMusic](https://www.tunemymusic.com/home) to sync to Tidal without thinking, and ended up with hundreds of unwanted playlists.  
This is a common DJ problem — plenty of sites handle transfers, but not deletion.
